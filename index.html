Overview

Carol Lopinski and Associates, LLC’s mission is to promote positive outcomes for all of Arizona’s children, families, and communities by championing quality family support and strengthening policies, training and practice. Carol Lopinski and Associates, LLC offers skills and experience that promote the well-being, strength and stability of Arizona’s children and families, and the professionals who serve them. As a trainer, Carol’s interactive style provides learning experiences that allow for engagement, self-reflection and practical application.

About

Carol Lopinski is the principal consultant of Carol Lopinski and Associates, LLC and has three decades of experience working with the private, non-profit and state government sectors. Carol has her Masters in Social Work (MSW), is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW), and is Certified by the Academy of Social Workers (ACSW). Carol Lopinski’s experience in developing, implementing and administering family support, child abuse prevention and early childhood programs has changed the landscape of services for children and families in Arizona. Carol provides innovative thought leadership and has an ability to consider state systems, organizations and the needs of families. This has resulted in state and community initiatives that strengthen policies and service provision for Arizona families.
