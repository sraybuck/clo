About

Bio

Carol Lopinski, MSW, LCSW, ACSW is the principal consultant of Carol Lopinski and Associates, LLC and has three decades of experience working with the private, non-profit and state government sectors. Carol Lopinski’s experience in developing, implementing and administering family support, child abuse prevention and early childhood programs has changed the landscape of services for children and families in Arizona. Her ability to consider state systems, organizations and the needs of families has resulted in state and community initiatives that strengthen policies and service provision for Arizona families. As a trainer, Carol’s interactive style provides learning experiences that allow for self-reflection and application opportunities.
