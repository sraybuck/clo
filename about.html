About

Carol Lopinski, MSW, LCSW, ACSW has worked in the private, non-profit and state government sectors for 34 years developing, implementing and administering family support, child abuse prevention and early childhood programs. Carol is now the principal consultant of Carol Lopinski and Associates, LLC, whose mission is “to promote positive outcomes for all of Arizona’s children, families, and communities by championing quality family support and strengthening policies, training and practice." Carol thinks strategically and considers the macro-needs of state systems; the mezzo needs of organizations and; the micro needs of families. Carol facilitates strategic planning, state and community initiatives and, trainings that are responsive, and promote the well-being, strength and stability of children and families and the professionals who serve them.
